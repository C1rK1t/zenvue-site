How we work

Built around your operation.

Every operation is different. We start by understanding yours, then design, install and support a monitoring solution that fits.

Every ZenVue deployment is bespoke: surveyed on site, installed and configured for your conditions, and fully supported for as long as you need it.

  1. 01

    Feasibility study

    We assess your sites, equipment and goals to confirm what is worth monitoring, and how.

  2. 02

    Site survey

    We visit on location to understand your real conditions, connectivity and the practicalities of each installation.

  3. 03

    Installation

    Our team installs and configures everything on site, so it works reliably in the field from day one.

  4. 04

    Integration

    We connect ZenVue to the systems that matter to you and shape the views around how you work.

  5. 05

    Ongoing support

    We keep it running and evolve it with you as your operation grows and changes.
